# Source policy

Priority order:
1. Government agencies and regulators
2. International organizations
3. Infrastructure operators and authorities
4. Primary datasets and official notices
5. Peer-reviewed research
6. Industry associations
7. Reputable news organizations
8. Vendor publications and social media leads

Vendor or media material may detect an event, but important conclusions require primary or independently corroborated evidence. Every material factual claim must retain a source URL, publication date, event/data period, and known limitation.
